Who I am.

I am an engineering student at Brown University with broad life experience and a diverse set of interests. I have a penchant for tinkering and a passion for improving the world and taking in as much of it as I can. I aspire to have a lasting, sustainable impact on the world through my work.

What I do.

I am in my sophomore year exploring different engineering tracks, but am primarily choosing between mechanical and electrical engineering. I have a passion for socially conscious engineering and sustainable enrgy. Right now, I split my time between class, rugby, being a Residential Peer Leader, working in the Brown Design Workshop, and building a CNC milling machine.

Work I've done.

Current Work

I am currently working on a CNC milling machine with a small group of peers. We plan to build the machine and develop a CAM software package that is user firendly, intuitive, and easy to learn. The software is being developed using openCASCADE, a software development kit intended for development of applications dealing with 3D CAD data.
