Who I am.

I am an engineering student at Brown University with broad life experience and a diverse set of interests. I have a penchant for tinkering and a passion for improving the world and taking in as much of it as I can. I aspire to have a lasting, sustainable impact on the world through my work.

What I do.

I am in my sophomore year exploring different engineering tracks, but am primarily choosing between mechanical and electrical engineering. I have a passion for socially conscious engineering, sustainable energy, and purpose-centered design. Right now, I split my time between class, rugby, being a Residential Peer Leader, working in the Brown Design Workshop, and building a CNC milling machine.
